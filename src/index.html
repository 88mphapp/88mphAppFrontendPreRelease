Getting started

How does 88mph work?

ðŸ’€ This project is in beta. Use at your own risk.

1. Deposit

Connect your Ethereum wallet to 88mph and deposit your DAI into the pool. Your deposit is locked in 88mph's pool for a minimum of 90 days. A higher lock period means a bigger amount of upfront fixed-rate interest. You can set up a higher lock period in the deposit modal.

2. Earn ...% upfront fixed-rate interest

Here's the cool part: You get the interest upfront! It's like your interest is travelling back from the future! While you get your fixed APY instantly, your deposit will generate interests during the lock period for the 88mph's pool at a variable APY on Compound protocol, insuring the stability of 88mph's pool.

3. Withdraw

When the lock period is over, withdraw your initial deposit. If you deposited 100.00 DAI, yeah, you get back 100.00 DAI.

88mph fees disclosures

88mph charges a 10% fee applied on your upfront interest.

88mph risks disclosures

If the interest rate drops too low compared with the interest rate at the time of the deposit, and many users try to withdraw at the same time, then 88mph could be temporarily insolvent, forcing users to wait a bit more to be able to withdraw their full deposit at the end of the lock period. Even if this risk exists, its probability is really small and 88mph is mitigating it with a sponsored deposit that allows the pool to reach a constant equilibrium while offering visibility to 88mph's sponsors.
